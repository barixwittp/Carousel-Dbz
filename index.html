Goku Black is an alternate version of Goku from a different timeline. Possessed by Zamasu, Goku Black wreaks havoc across the multiverse, causing immense destruction. His Super Saiyan Rose transformation makes him one of the deadliest foes.

FLYING NIMBUS
MAGICAL ITEM
The Flying Nimbus is a magical cloud used by Goku for travel. Given by Master Roshi, this cloud can only be ridden by those with a pure heart, and it plays a key role in Goku’s early adventures.
